Heart disease generally refers to the conditions that involve narrowed or blocked blood vessels that can lead to a heart attack, chest pain(angina) or stroke. Other heart conditions, such as those that affect your heart's muscle, valves or rhythm, also are considered forms of heart disease.

Patients with liver disease have been continuously increasing because of excessive consumption of alcohol,inhalation of harmful gases, intake of contaminated foods, pickles and drugs.

Diabetes is a chronic disease or group of metabolic diseases where a person suffers from extended level of blood glucose in the body, which is either the insulin production is inadequate, or because the body's cells do not respond properly to insulin.

Breast cancer (BC) is one of the most common cancers among women worldwide, represnting the majority of new cancer cases and cancer related deaths according to the global statistics, making it a significant public health problem in today's society.

Symptoms

Heart Disease Symptopms

Chest pain , chest tightness, chest pressure, chest discomfort, shortness of breadth, pain in the neck,jaw, upper abdomen or back.

Liver Disease Symptopms

Abdominal pain, swelling in the legs and ankles, itchy skin, dark urine color, chronic fatigue, nausea, or vomiting, loss of appetite.

Diabetes Symptopms

Urinating often, feeling very thirsty, feeling very hungry, extreme fatigue, blurry vision, tingling or numbness in the hands, cuts/bruises that are slow to heal.

Brest Cancer Symptopms

Irritated or itchy breasts, change in breast colour, increase in breast size or shape(over a short period of time), changes in touch(may feel hard, tender or warm), peeling or flaking of the nipple skin, breast lump or thickening, redness or pitting of the breast skin.
